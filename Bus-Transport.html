About MBMT

MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) (Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Transport), also known as MBMT, is a public transportation provider in Mumbai that operates Bus routes since 2006.

The MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) has 27 Bus routes in Mumbai with 345 Bus stops.

Coverage Area

The MBMT (Mira Bhayandar Municipal Transport) has Bus routes operating across Mumbai including: Mira Bhayandar, Borivali West.
